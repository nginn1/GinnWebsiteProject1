The Top 10 Games of the Year!

1.Luigi's Mansion
Luigi's Mansion is a great game much like resident evil where you traverse a big spooky mansion and find keys to get to the end, except with cute ghosts and you suck them up with a vaccum instead of shooting them. 10/10
2. Zelda
Zelda is pretty though to be honest not as good as Donkey Kong but still pretty good. 10/10
3. Fallout
Fallout is a really good game with tons to do and places to explore. Highl recommended game. 9/10
4. Kirby
Running around as a tiny pink ball and sucking up bad guys is really fun, you should try it. 8/10
5. Animal Crossing
This is a game that took me a while to get into but it's very relaxing and rewarding when you really put the time into it. 8/10
6. Halo
I've never actually played this game but I heard it was really good. 7/10
7. Star Wars
I made this game up but there are plenty of star wars games on the market for you to enjoy so just pick one. ??/10
8. Nintendogs
This is only at 8 on this list because I couldn't get a shih-tzu. 7/10
9. Oblivion
This game is great a lot like fallout but in a midievil fantasy world. The people are scary looking though. 7/10
10. Starfield
Another game like Fallout and Oblivion but this time in space! You can build and fly ships, visit tons of different planets and fight bad guys. Good game. 7/10
