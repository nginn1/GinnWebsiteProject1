Classic Fortnite Season Brings New Life to the Game!

Fortnite has brought back an older season to draw in older player and it has seemingly worked with the game reaching an overall 44.7 Million players on the first night of release.

Freddy Fazbear is Hiring Once Again!

A brand new Five Nights at Freddy's game is set to release toward the end of next month. It is a second installment to their last VR endeavor "Five Nights at Freddys: Help Wanted". Developed by Steel Wool Games, the developers of the last "Help Wanted" as well as "Five Nights at Freddys: Security Breach", this game is set to release in mid to late December.
